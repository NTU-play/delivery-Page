Delivery rules

  1. The delivery fee is $1.
  2. The delivery pick-up point and destination must be located within NTU.
  3. The delivery service is only available for item not greater than 5kg.
  4. The delivery service is only available for item which is listed in the NTUPLAY platform.
  5. The delivery time is fall between 12 PM to 7 PM daily.
  6. We encourage buyers to pay the delivery fee and the amount of the item via Paylah. We do not accept cash payment.
  7. Only paid delivery orders will be processed.

Before you proceed to the delivery form, make sure that you have done the following:

  1. Purchase the item.
  2. Pay the amount of the item and delivery fee. We will ask you to upload the e-receipt
